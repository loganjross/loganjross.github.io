Slide

A user-focused, effortless web3 wallet.

I came into Slide with the purpose of cleaning up the frontend codebase and revamping the UI/UX for the embedded wallet widget. We've made an incredibly smooth and easy to use web3 user experience, all available from a simple email-based login. One of the major frictions Slide solves is allowing easy on and off ramping from fiat to cryptocurrencies, as well as the NFT Checkout feature allowing a user to purchase an NFT, direct from a marketplace with their debit/credit card. Jet Protocol

A DeFi protocol built on the Solana blockchain.

As the first employee of Jet, I was tasked with creating our user interface. I wanted the product to match our mission within DeFi as a whole—to disrupt the status quo. We created something not only unique, but also powerful and intuitive to use. Considering the end goal of decentralization, we wanted to ensure the development of the protocol and interface well into the future. This lead us to create a TypeScript library for easy integration with and use of our on-chain programs, along with a stylesheet package to perpetuate our unique designs. Dealr

A DeFi protocol built on the Solana blockchain.

Dealr is an app that makes the day to day lives of independent car dealers a heck of a lot easier. It's a CRM, a marketing tool, and a sales tool all in one. It's a one stop shop for all of your car dealer needs. This was my first programming job, starting as a junior developer and cutting my teeth on things outside my normal scope such as PHP and MySQL databases. I solved bugs, introduced new features and worked on a widely varying tech stack, all while learning a lot about the car industry. JavaScript HTML CSS React Twig PHP MySQL
